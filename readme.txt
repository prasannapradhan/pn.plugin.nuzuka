== Description ==

Nuzuka Beta is [the most powerful](https://nuzuka.com) website enquiry capture system. 
Contact forms for enquiry usually take more than a minute to fill. Nuzuka creates one click contact widgets which take less than a minute to build and less than 5 secs for a customer enquiry.
Whether you want to monitor your customer visits to the website pages or generate equiries for your items. Nuzuka is the right platform to address that.
Nuzuka utilizes advanced AI and Prediction techniques to predict your enquiry conversion probability. The ratio starts at 30% and ends at 100%.
Nuzuka focuses on gathering Customer Intelligence more than what the Customer provides. 

- **Nuzuka includes a Site Manager to manage all your site pages and display enquiry widgets seamlessly.
- **Nuzuka ensures that any equiry you receive is 100% spam proof and you do not waste time running after false leads.
- **Nuzuka makes enquiries relevant i.e. you know exactly what the customer wants from you.
- **Nuzuka contact widgets are a visual delight and can be changed dynamically from the backend no actual wordpress expertize is required.
- **Nuzuka integrates with Google Signin and Apple Signin for one click customer authentication.
- **Nuzuka integrates with Woocommerce API seamlessly to integrate all the inventory and generate bulk enquiries.

- Showcase physical and digital goods in a contact widget and generate enquiries 
- **Your website remains the same but the content can change as you want.

Built-in tools and popular integrations help you efficiently manage your business operations. Site Manager, Visitor Analytics, Customer CRM, Enquiry CRM, Inventory Management, Woocommerce Integration all are inbuilt in one plugin.

= Grow your business, add features, and monitor website on the go

Nuzuka means business. Your customers are forever yours. Nuzuka keeps track of who visited earlier, on what services and what were their previous enquiries.

Nuzuka sends email updates on with geo tagging, customer info and conversion coefficient.

= Why Nuzuka will work in your site =

Nuzuka is driven by a restful APP which shares all the load externally. Whatever may be the issues at your hosting level Nuzuka will never stop serving your customers.

- [Do less and get more] Nuzuka employs auto content enrichment  
- [No Spam] Nuzuka widgets are hack proof they cannot be spoofed or fooled to enter spam
- [Dynamic Content] Content is modular and is driven by a time tested inventory management module
- [Display Handlers] Nuzuka not only which page got loaded but also the page position and relevancy
- [Speed of Deployment] Nuzuka everything is by configuration a lot of stress has been given to make content display automatic.

The core platform is tested rigorously and supported by a dedicated development team.

== Installation ==

= Minimum Requirements =

* PHP 7.2 or greater is recommended
* MySQL 5.6 or greater is recommended

There is no specific server requirements. Nuzuka should work with your site as is. 

= Automatic installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option -- WordPress will handle the file transfer, and you won’t need to leave your web browser. To do an automatic install of Nuzuka, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu, and click “Add New.”
 
In the search field type “Nuzuka,” then click “Search Plugins.” Once you’ve found us,  you can view details about it such as the point release, rating, and description. Most importantly of course, you can install it by! Click “Install Now,” and WordPress will take it from there.

= Manual installation =

Manual installation method requires downloading the Nuzuka Wordpress plugin and uploading it to your web server via your favorite FTP application. The WordPress codex contains [instructions on how to do this here](https://wordpress.org/support/article/managing-plugins/#manual-plugin-installation).

= Updating =

Automatic updates should work smoothly, but we still recommend you back up your site.
